Concept

In this new eras of globalization, travelling has gained decent popularity. However, despite most avoid travel agencies to get a self-decided trip, people fail to make the best use of time and money. Tripago, combining all convenient pricing and scheduling system, gives you the best platform to make your ideal trip come true.

Tripago, designed for the best trip arrangements.

What is tripago?
Tripago users form a group to discuss and share tripping data online, while tripago transform scrap information to a well-organized schedule. Using co-editing, online calculation to estaimate price and time spent, then vote for the best results.

Wether you're preparing for your next trip, or finding examples of other trips taken. Tripago will realize it. Make it happen. Be your own boss.
Why tripago?
  1. Co-editing through tripago reduces wasted time between group members discussion.
  2. Easy access to all data collected, including hotels, restaurants, transportaion, and tourist spots.
  3. Preview system to easily estimate the time and money spent on every combination of the data collected.
  4. Sharing and viewing other examples can give you the best advice for your next trip.
How to tripago?
Four stages to accomplish an ideal schedule.
  1. Data collection.
  2. Vote for the best combination.
  3. Schedule comfirmed.
  4. Share and comment.
